Ecology Projects

The Blandford Nature Center is an incredible place for anyone to enjoy or learn about nature even in an urban area like Grand Rapids, MI. I was fortunate enough to help care for their wonderful nature "ambassadors" for several years as a volunteer, caring for the spectrum of snakes to bobcats. Most of my time was spent doing meal preparation for the carnivorous birds or the herps (reptiles and amphibians), each with their own quirks and personalities. You haven't quite lived until you've gotten into a giant bird cage full of turkey vultures, hands full of food (they're incredibly docile, just excited for dinner!)
