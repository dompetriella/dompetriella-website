About Me

Hi, I'm Dominic Petriella!

I'm a programmer located in Grand Rapids, Michigan

I specialize in web development and can't wait to help you with web-based solutions!

Background

To say I'm a Jack-of-all-trades would be an understatement. My curiousity of how the world and people tick has led me down many unique paths and bodies of knowledge that made me an excellent problem solver.

I've tinkered with computers since I was a little kid, fascinated by a seemingly invisible world that existed in the (at the time, very boxy) monitor. This fascination of a tiny world translated into a love for biology in high school and college getting my degree in Cell and Molecular Biology. During this time, I also became interested in not only how the world works, but also people, and recieved my minor in Communications. I eventually stumbled my way back into computer science after all these years, armed with all the knowledge from ecletic fields, excited to help design websites that incorporate my knowledge of programming, my enthusiasm for personal communications, and passion for solving problems.

All work and no play makes a Jack-of-all-Trades a dull boy- I also have some of my side projects on this site here

I got my start in coding making silly side projects, and that hasn't changed. I make little apps to assist everyday life or bring a little enjoyment to it. Some are hosted here on my site, while some still in progress are found on my Github or on my Replit

Long before I was dabbling in code, I was dabbling (and continue to dabble) in music! Compositions of mine show up on this site periodically, along with some in more public spaces like Spotify. Check out all that noise here

I still love working in science, and have some of my ecology work available on this page. When I'm not in the office chair, I'm usually lost in the woods somewhere (purposefully, of course).
